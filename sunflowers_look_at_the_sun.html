This is the first day I'm writing something here, despite I've made this page long time ago, I couldn't encourage myself to work on it and write my thoughts anywere. That's it, I will write anything I will need to share with someone, something like a diary. Maybe one day I will post my stories and poems here, but it's an another topic. Maybe one day I will totally change or delete this archive, I don't like keeping memories. The primary reason for this is to improve my writing, English and Esperanto skills. I may make some mistakes in both languages, so I will try to learn the grammar simultaneously. I'm a hobbist writer, I was writing in many languages, the funniest was writing in Toki Pona, language with 100 words that I've already forgot. I find it hard to express my ideas and emotions, telling interestingly and funny, so writing can improve that. I've learned many languages in my life, I completely forgot the Japanese and Arabic, even if I wasn't good at them, it's sad how fast the skills fade out. I was learning Greek for several months and I was good at it, but I found out that I don't progress anymore, it became boring, so I took a break for few months. I still remember it and can talk with some effort. The idea of combining writing and Esperanto came to me recently. Keeping a diary makes you talk about your interests, the things you want to hear and do, so it builds an ideal vocabulary. I will try to translate every chapter into Esperanto and write the words I didn't know, even if it will have many grammatical mistakes, I will fix them with time. If you talk, think, write about an urban life, you won't need to know all names of vegetables and other nature, the oposite if you mostly read about gardening. Learning a language is a though thing, a hobby that many people never accomplish. The language of hope will be my first step to end the endless circle of failures.
